Draw a digit between 0 and 9 above and then the CNN model predicts your hand-drawn number. 
    A neural network will predict your digit in the blue square above. 
    Your image is 784 pixels (= 28 rows by 28 columns with black=1 and white=0). 
    Those 784 features get fed into a 5 layer neural network; Input:784 - MaxPool:32 - MaxPool:64 - AvgPool:128 - Dense:128 - Dense:10 - Output. 
    The net has 111,370 parameters and was trained on 60,000 images from the MNIST Dataset.
    It achieves 98.2% accuracy on test-set of 10,000 images.
